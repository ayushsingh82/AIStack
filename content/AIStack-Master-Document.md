# Long-Term Vision

The goal is to build **the roadmap.sh of AI Engineering**—a free, community-driven platform where developers can learn everything from transformer fundamentals to production AI infrastructure. Every topic should combine theory, official documentation, practical demos, projects, production insights, and reusable content so learners can not only understand AI systems but also build, deploy, and teach them. Over time, the platform should grow into a comprehensive reference for AI engineers at every stage of their journey.
